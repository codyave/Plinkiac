'''Richard William Thomas Evans III''' is an American actor, writer, film and video game critic, and internet personality. Evans is best known for starring in the [[Red Letter Media]] productions [[Space Cop]] and Gorilla Interrupted, as well as the web television series Half in the Bag, Best of the Worst, re:View, and Nerd Crew on the video platform YouTube. His other project includes reviews of PC and console games for the web series Previously Recorded.
